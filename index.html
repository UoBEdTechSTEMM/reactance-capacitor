Capacitive Reactance

This app shows a graph of the reactance of a capacitor against the capacitance. The reactance is given by,

\[X_C = \frac{1}{2\pi f C}\]

where \(f\) is the frequency (in \(Hz\)), and \(C\) is the capacitance (in Farads). Note the asymptotic behaviour of the reactance as the capacitance goes to zero.

Shift-click and drag on the graph to pan.
